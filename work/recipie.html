Sambar Recipie

About Sambar

Sambar is a staple in many south indian families and is a protein rich, nutritious meal that can be enjoyed on its own or with rice. One could compare sambar to stew and can be made in various ways. So get creative! and add whatever variations of vegetables that you think would go well. Below is a traditional sambar recipie that was perfected by my grandmother. I am going to use a shortcut, and use the sambar powder tht my grandma gave me, but you can make your own. More on that later! Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • potatoes

Directions
boil the lentils for an hour in medium heat add turmeric powder

Saute the onions first and then the other vegetables
soak tamrind in a bowl of water to extract juice
